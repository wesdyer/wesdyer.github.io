## 2024-05-18 - Wordle Icon-Only and Dynamically Injected Button A11y
**Learning:** Icon-only buttons (like the `bar_chart` statistics button and the `close` button in the stats modal) must have explicit `aria-label`s since they lack inner text content, otherwise they will be read as blank by screen readers. Furthermore, dynamically injected buttons (like those on a virtual keyboard) must also be carefully styled for keyboard focus visibility (`focus-visible:ring-2`) and have proper `aria-label`s (like `aria-label="Backspace"`) to ensure the interactive elements are accessible to keyboard users and screen readers, respectively.
**Action:** Always check `index.html` headers/modals and any DOM generation logic (like `document.createElement('button')` in JavaScript) to ensure `aria-label` and `focus-visible` Tailwind classes are correctly applied.
